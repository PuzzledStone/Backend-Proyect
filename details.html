Bibimbap

225

destacado

Difficulty Category Total time Cooking time Portions Occasion
Medium Diner 1 hr 30 min 1 Family

Descripcion

Bibimbap is a traditional Korean dish that is made up of white rice and many vegetables, to which protein such as meat and egg is added, as well as spicy sauce. But currently there are many varieties of this complete dish,ç including vegetarian and vegan versions.

Ingredientes

1 cup Short Grain Rice
2 fried eggs
bibimbap sauce
2 tablespoons gochujang
1 spoon of sugar
1 tablespoon sesame oil
1 tablespoon of water
1 teaspoon rice vinegar
1 teaspoon sesame seeds
1 teaspoon minced garlic
Vegetables
4 ounces of spinach
4 ounces bean sprouts
4 oz julienned carrot
4 oz Persian or English cucumber, thinly sliced ​​and cut in half
4 oz zucchini cut into sticks
2 teaspoons minced garlic
2 green onions, chopped
2 teaspoons sesame oil
1 teaspoon sesame seeds
salt
Beef
1/2 pound ribeye, cut into thin slices
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons brown sugar
2 teaspoons sesame oil
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 tablespoon grated Asian pear

Preparation

Step 1
-First, wash the rice until the water runs clear. Then cover it with water and let it sit in a pot for 30 minutes.
Step 2
-After this time, cover the pot and cook over medium-low heat for 18 minutes. Then turn off the heat and fluff the rice to release the steam. Keep covered and reserve.
Step 3
-Mix all the meat ingredients in a bowl and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
Step 4
- Also mix the bibimbap sauce ingredients and set aside.
Step 5
- Next, add 1/4 teaspoon salt to the zucchini, 1/4 teaspoon salt to the cucumbers, and 1/8 teaspoon salt to the carrots. Mix everything until well combined and let it sit for 10 minutes.
Step 6
-Wash the spinach, blanch it in boiling water for 20 seconds and immediately immerse it in cold water. Thoroughly remove any moisture, cut the spinach ball in half and season with 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic, 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil, a pinch of salt and 1 teaspoon chopped green onion. Mix well and let the result rest.
Step 7
- On the other hand, wash the bean sprouts, discard the golden brown parts, blanch them in boiling water, cover the pot and cook over medium heat for 3 or 4 minutes. After this time, rinse them with cold water and season with 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic, 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil, a pinch of salt, 1 teaspoon chopped green onion and 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds. Set the result aside.
Step 8
- Next, discard the water extracted from the cucumbers and gently remove the moisture with absorbent paper. Season with 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic, 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil, 1 teaspoon chopped green onion, and 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds, and set aside.
Step 9
- Also discard the water extracted from the zucchini and gently remove the moisture. Then lightly fry the vegetable over medium-low heat and mix it together with 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic, 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil, 1 teaspoon chopped green onion, and 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds. Stir fry for 2-3 minutes then set aside.
Step 10
- Finally, discard the water extracted from the carrots and remove the moisture. Then sauté them in a little vegetable oil for 2-3 minutes.
Step 11
- Then fry the marinated meat in a little vegetable oil over medium heat. Cook it to your liking and set aside.
Step 12
- Place the vegetables, beef and 2 or 3 tablespoons of sauce on the rice.
Step 13
- Finally, fry an egg and place it right in the center of the bowl.
Step 14
- Mix everything together and enjoy this delicious dish!
